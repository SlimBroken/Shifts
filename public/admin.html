📋 Schedule Generation Rules & Constraints

🚫 Hard Constraints (Never Violated)

  • Maximum 2 night shifts per week - Workers cannot be assigned more than 2 night shifts in any single week
  • One shift per day - Workers cannot work multiple shifts on the same day
  • 8-hour minimum break - At least 8 hours must pass between the end of one shift and start of the next
  • Availability only - Workers are only assigned to shifts they marked as available
  • Approved workers only - Only workers with approved submissions can be scheduled

🎯 Optimization Goals

  • Fair shift distribution - Balance total shifts across all workers
  • Morning shift priority - Ensure every worker gets at least one morning shift
  • Night shift balance - Distribute night shifts fairly while respecting the 2-per-week limit
  • Premium shift fairness - Distribute premium weekend shifts (Fri evening → Sat evening) equally
  • Minimize consecutive days - Avoid scheduling workers on consecutive days when possible

🕐 Shift Information

  • Morning (בוקר): 07:00 - 15:00
  • Evening (ערב): 15:00 - 23:00
  • Night (לילה): 23:00 - 07:00 (next day)
  • Premium Period: Friday evening through Saturday evening (higher pay)
  • Total shifts: 42 shifts across 2 weeks (3 shifts × 7 days × 2 weeks)

⚙️ Generation Process

  1. Initial Pass: Score and assign workers to shifts based on availability and constraints
  2. Load Balancing: Prefer workers with fewer total shifts to ensure fairness
  3. Constraint Checking: Verify all hard constraints before assignment
  4. Empty Shift Filling: Second pass to fill any remaining empty shifts
  5. Coverage Optimization: Maximize total shift coverage while respecting all rules

📊 Success Metrics

  • Coverage Percentage: Ratio of filled shifts to total shifts
  • Fairness Score: How evenly shifts are distributed
  • Constraint Compliance: 100% adherence to all hard constraints
  • Worker Satisfaction: Everyone gets preferred shifts when possible
  • Night Shift Distribution: Verified ≤2 night shifts per worker per week

⚠️ Important Notes

  • Lock Before Generation: Preferences are automatically locked during schedule generation
  • Real-time Validation: All constraints are checked in real-time during assignment
  • Detailed Logging: Check browser console (F12) for detailed generation analysis
  • Manual Override: Admin can manually adjust schedules after generation if needed
  • Export Ready: Generated schedules can be copied directly to Excel
